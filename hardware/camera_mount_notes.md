# Camera Mount and Capture Notes

## Current Setup

- Processor: Raspberry Pi 3 Model B
- Camera: Raspberry Pi Camera Module
- Capture orientation: overhead top-down view
- Target: full 15x15 Gomoku board visible in frame

## Mounting Requirements

The mount should:

- keep the camera stable during testing
- maintain a repeatable top-down angle
- ensure the full board remains visible
- minimise perspective distortion
- avoid large changes in camera height between captures

## Calibration Considerations

Camera and board alignment affects:

- grid mapping
- row/column coordinate estimation
- edge/corner accuracy
- stone localisation consistency

Therefore, after calibration images are collected, the camera should not be moved unless re-calibration is performed.
